Sit to Stand

Reminder

1. Play the video instruction as your reference.

2. Then press the 1-minute timer once you are ready.

3. Ensure that the chair you are using is stable and placed on a flat surface to avoid slipping.

4. Sit towards the front edge of the chair with your feet flat on the floor and shoulder-width apart.

5. Keep your back straight and engage your core muscles throughout the exercise.

6. If you feel any discomfort or dizziness, stop immediately and consult a healthcare professional.
